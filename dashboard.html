      Who we are

      Social Impact

      CulinaryOS is a social impact project preserving the food culture of our people — making it accessible to generations to come.

      NOW — DATA PHASE

      Building the archive

      We're gathering Nigeria's dishes, tribe by tribe, state by state. Our social impact work grows directly out of this archive as it matures.

      Campaign

      Save The Boy Chef

      Encouraging boys and young men to embrace kitchen life — breaking the stigma that cooking isn't for them, one classroom and one community at a time.

      Roadshow

      Boys Can Cook

      A touring food-show format bringing live cooking, storytelling and heritage dishes into communities — planned as our archive and partnerships grow.

      AHEAD

      Scaling the impact

      As the data layer matures into full infrastructure (V3), more of what we give back to the community will be funded directly by what we've preserved.
